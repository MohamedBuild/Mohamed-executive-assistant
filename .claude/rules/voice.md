# Voice and Communication Rules

## Internal Tone (just us)
Informal. Direct. No ceremony — get to the point.

## External Tone (client / public-facing)
Professional but warm. Confident without being stiff. Write like a sharp human, not a company.

## Formatting Preferences
Short paragraphs, straight to the point. No bullet overload. Use tables when comparing things. Bullets only when a list genuinely reads better than prose.

## Pet Peeves — Never Do These
- No tech jargon — if a normal person wouldn't say it, don't write it
- No filler words or throat-clearing ("As you know...", "It's worth noting that...")
- No corporate speak ("leverage", "synergy", "circle back", "touch base")
- No emojis
- No padding — every sentence earns its place
