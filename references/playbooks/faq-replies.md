# FAQ Reply Templates

## How to use
Find the question. Pick the tone. Copy the version you need (Gmail or WhatsApp). Personalise the [BRACKETS] and send.

**Tone guide:**
- **Cold lead** — someone considering AGENCINA, hasn't committed yet
- **Existing client** — current or past client, already knows the context
- **Refund / friction** — pushback, complaint, cancellation, or anything that needs careful handling

**Fill these in before using (one-time setup):**
- `[PRICING_RANGE]` — your typical project range, e.g. "$2,000–$8,000"
- `[PORTFOLIO_LINK]` — link to case studies or examples
- `[TIMELINE_RANGE]` — typical delivery window, e.g. "3–6 weeks"
- `[REFUND_POLICY]` — your policy in plain language
- `[AVAILABILITY]` — your current capacity note
